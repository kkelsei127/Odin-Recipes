Hot Chocolate

Description

For the "ooh baby its cold outside" type of day, cozy up with this decadent cup of chocolate

Reach perfection level when you pair with any of the following: cold weather, snow, Holiday music or homemade marshmallows!

Ingredients

  • 12 ounces semi-seet chocolate, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 6 cups half and half
  • HOLIDAY CHEER!

Steps

  1. Step 1:
    Place chocolate, butter, and vanilla extract in the top of a double boiler over simmering water. Stir frequently, scraping dow nthe sides with a rubber spatula to avoid scorching. until chocolate is melted and completely smooth, about 5 minutes. Add half and half slowly to avoid cooling chocolate mixture too much, stirring while you add it. Heat until hot but not boiling.
  2. Step 2:
    Pour into your favorite holiday mug, crank the holidy tunes and enjoy!
