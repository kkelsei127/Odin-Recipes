Mocha Coffee

Description

Did someone say chocolate? Chocolate AND coffee? Where do I sign up?

Elevate your morning pick me up with just a few ingredients!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup hot brewed coffee
  • 1 tablespoons unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 tablespoon white sugar
  • 2 tablespoons milk
  • HAPPINESS!

Steps

  1. Step 1:
    Pour hot coffee into a mug. Stir in cocoa, sugar and milk.
  2. Step 2:
    Find a quiet and comfy spot, sit back and relax while waking up with the perfect morning beverage!
